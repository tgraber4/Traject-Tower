IT Internship - Application Development

Location: Mt. Sterling, IL or Chesterfield, MO

Department: Information Technology

Reports To: IT Manager

Pay Range: $17 - $19

You’re a creative, a tech-wiz, and detail oriented. You think logically but also dream up ideas outside of the box. You are curious, a problem-solver, enjoy tinkering with technology, and provide excellent customer service. If this sounds like you, Dot Foods wants you on our team.

As an IT Intern for software development, you will train, learn, and work with the IT department to gain understanding of workflows, processes, and how IT interacts with other parts of the business. You’ll complete a minimum of one assigned project for review and possible implementation, in addition to gaining valuable professional work experience, knowledge, and skills.

WHAT YOU’LL DO

Job-shadow within the IT department to deliver solutions and improve functionality
Lead and own assigned project(s) within application development
Participate in a team project with other interns
Participate in activities related to Agile software development methodologies
Problem-solve through research, collecting data, and analyzing to determine best processes and methods
Review, update, and track a variety of reports
Present project information at the culmination of the internship to communicate research, findings, analysis, and any recommendations
Apply lean thinking to identify and eliminate waste in all areas of the position

WORK STATEMENT

Dot Foods is not currently hiring individuals for this position who now or in the future require

YOU MUST HAVE

Good standing as full-time student actively pursuing a Bachelor’s degree at an accredited college/university
Minimum cumulative G.P.A. of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale
Ability to manage multiple projects and priorities
Proficiency in Microsoft Office programs
Creative & innovative thinking

YOU MAY ALSO HAVE

Degree work towards a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, MIS, or a related field
Professional verbal, written, and interpersonal communication skills
Programming and database experience or aptitude
Exposure to one or more: Microsoft C#, .Net, SQL

ROLE SPECIFICS

Work a minimum of 350 hours during internship tenure
Attend Dot specific business and personal development intern training events
Potential for minimal overnight travel; ability to travel without restriction by all modes of transportation
